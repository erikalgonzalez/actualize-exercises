Magical Pancake Recipe

These pancakes will make your mornings magical.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup magic flour
  • 2 enchanted eggs
  • 1/2 cup fairy dust sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract from magical vanilla beans
  • 1 1/2 cup dragon milk
  • Butter from a unicorn for greasing the pan
  • Optional: Rainbow sprinkles for topping or to put in the pancakes
  • Optional: Food coloring for diferent color pancakes

Instructions

  1. Combine the magic flour, enchanted eggs, fairy dust sugar, and the vanilla extract from magical vanilla beans in a mixing bowl.
  2. Gradually add the dragon milk while mixing until the batter is smooth.
  3. Heat the pan of you choice over meduim heat and grease with the butter from a unicorn.
  4. Pour a scoop of batter onto the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  7. If you want diferent color pancakes then separate the batter into diferent bowls and add different color food coloring in each bowl.
  8. If you want rainbow sprinkles in the pancakes then mix them in the batter, if you don't want them in the pancakes then sprinkle them on the cooked pancakes.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy your magical breakfast.
